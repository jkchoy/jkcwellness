after care rules

Please let your hair grow for approximately two weeks before your appointment.

Avoid sun exposure 24 hours prior to and 24 hours after waxing.

Minimize caffeine on the day of your visit—it can stimulate the nervous system and increase discomfort.

Steer clear of heat and sweat for 24 hours post-waxing. This means no workouts, sauna, steam room, hot tub, or beach trips—sweat can carry bacteria into pores and irritate freshly waxed skin.
