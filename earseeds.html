ear seeds

non-invasive (needless)

and pretty much trouble-free

Ear seeds are tiny Vaccaria seeds or little stainless-steel pellets stuck on your ears with clear adhesive tape.

They're placed on specific ear spots—kind of like foot reflexology—where the pressure helps ease symptoms throughout your body.

They stay put for 3-5 days. If they fall off, no worries. If they're still hanging on, gently peel them off yourself.

Ear seed therapy works best over time—the gains stack. Spacing your sessions right helps keep the momentum going.
